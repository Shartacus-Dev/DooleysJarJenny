Why the Jar Jenny?

Opening a jar shouldn’t feel like wrestling a jenny donkey—adorable, but oh-so-stubborn! The Jar Jenny grips with gentle strength, making it easy for everyone in the family to open jars, bottles, and more—without the hastle.

  • Handmade in the USA
  • Metal teeth that give just the right bite.
  • Easy-turn design open anything with just one twist.

Installation

Installation

The Jar Jenny is designed to open jars for a life time all from the bottom of your cabinet! To install your very own just follow the steps below:

1. Pick the Perfect Spot

Find a cozy spot underneath your upper cabinets—somewhere easy to reach and right where you’d naturally grab a jar.

2. Mount with Confidence

Line up Jar Jenny with the narrow end facing you (that’s the "bite" zone!). Use the included screws to secure it in place.

3. That’s It—You’re Ready to Twist & Shout!

Grab a jar (new and sealed for the full wow-factor), slide the lid into Jar Jenny’s “V” teeth, gently push up, and give it a twist. Pop! Just like that, the lid’s off—and you're a kitchen hero.

Pro Tip!

The provided hardware is perfect for standard ½" thick cabinet floors. Just give it a quick peek as you install to make sure the screws stay snug and don’t poke through the top.

It's so simple, so smooth, and so out of sight, you'll find yourself using Jar Jenny more than you ever expected. Whether it's pasta night, pickles, or peanut butter—she's always there, quietly saving the day.
